Section 3: Decoding the Technology

Understand the pros and cons of each purification method to see why a multi-stage RO system is the safest bet for most of Hyderabad.

Technology Removes Key Pro Key Con
Reverse Osmosis (RO) Dissolved Solids, Heavy Metals, Chemicals Most comprehensive purification Wastes water, strips minerals
Ultraviolet (UV) Inactivates Bacteria & Viruses Disinfects without chemicals Doesn't remove any physical impurities
Ultrafiltration (UF) Suspended Solids, some Bacteria No electricity needed, keeps minerals Can't remove dissolved solids (TDS)

Critical Feature: TDS Controller

Modern RO purifiers should have a TDS controller. This feature re-adds essential minerals back into the purified water, improving taste and health benefits. Consider it a must-have.

Section 7: Final Verdict & Smart Shopping

Your choice depends on your TDS test results and your priority: saving money upfront or ensuring easier long-term DIY.

Choose the Aquaguard Delight NXT if...

...your primary goal is the **lowest possible initial cost**. It's an unbeatable value proposition, but be prepared to research how to bypass its service lock for future DIY savings.

Choose the Kent Supreme Lite if...

...you're willing to pay a slight premium for **simpler long-term DIY management**. Its easier alarm reset makes it the more straightforward choice for committed self-servicers.

Pro Shopping Tips

  • Buy from reputable online retailers like Amazon, Flipkart, or Croma for the best prices and return policies.
  • Look for deals during major sales events like Prime Day or Diwali sales.
  • Stack offers: Use bank card discounts and No-Cost EMI options to save even more.
  • Consider exchange offers on brand websites if you have an old purifier.
